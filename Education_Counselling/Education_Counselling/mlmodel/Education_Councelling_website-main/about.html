Welcome to EduMap- Discover. Connect. Succeed.


EduMap is a collaborative learning platform dedicated to bridging the gap in educational resources and making quality education accessible to everyone. Designed with a focus on B.Tech Computer Science students, EduMap harnesses the power of AI to match learners with personalized learning paths, experienced mentors, and supportive peer study groups. Our goal is to provide students with the tools they need to succeed in both academic and professional realms. Through our platform, students can connect with mentors who bring real-world expertise, access tailored resources that align with their individual learning goals, and interact with a vibrant community of peers. EduMap also features an AI-powered chatbot for instant support, answering academic queries, and guiding students along their learning journey. Join EduMap to discover new learning opportunities, connect with the right mentors, and succeed in achieving your academic and career aspirations.
